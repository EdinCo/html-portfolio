My favourite movies

The list of three best movies

1. Amanet

"Amanet" is a film about family life and customs in Bosnia. It can still only be seen in cinemas.

2. Deset u pola

Unlike Hollywood films, "Deset u pola" is a film where you cannot predict the events in the film, nor the ending. The film is a story about Sarajevo kebabs and the eternal Sarajevo question: Which kebabs are the best?

3. Alita - Battle angel

Alita is a cyber-punk film based on Japanese comics. The film is interesting and it is a metaphor for the struggle for social influence.
